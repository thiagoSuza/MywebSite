About the Studio

A growing independent studio focused on building games with Unity for multiple platforms.

Charlotte Cat Games is an independent game development studio focused on creating games with Unity. The studio works on both experimental prototypes and commercial projects, always exploring new gameplay ideas, different genres and polished player experiences.

Projects include 2D and 3D games for Desktop, Android and WebGL, with experience in action, racing, arcade, puzzle and educational games. The goal is to create games that are fun, clear and technically solid.

The studio also develops online multiplayer experiences using Photon PUN2, making it possible to build connected gameplay systems for players across different platforms.

Studio Focus

  • Unity development for PC, Android and WebGL
  • 2D and 3D games across multiple genres
  • Online multiplayer with Photon PUN2
  • Prototypes, published titles and gameplay experiments

Featured Game

A highlighted commercial release from the studio.

Steam Release

Martian Conquer

Martian Conquer is a turn based strategy game with economic management, developed by Charlotte Cat Games and released on Steam.

In the game, the player must invade and conquer Earth by managing resources, expanding influence and sending cargo ships to support the conquest.

The project combines tactical decision making, progression and economy systems, making it one of the main commercial titles of the studio.
